## 📖 Abstract

TRT is a quantum gravity framework grounded in informational thermodynamics.  
The **modular Hamiltonian** \( K_{\rho_n} = -\ln \rho_n \) of each node’s reduced state is identified as the **physical Hamiltonian at Planck scale** (Postulate II), yielding the Energy–Information Identity \( \langle H_n \rangle = S(\rho_n) \) in Planck units.

Quantum coherence is measured relative to a **node‑local geometric reference basis** \( \mathcal{B}_n \) (the intertwiner basis of LQG), producing a non‑trivial decomposition:

- **\( I_{\text{cl}} \)**: classical information → mass  
- **\( C_{\mathcal{B}} \)**: geometric coherence → radiative sector  

Spacetime emerges selectively on **condensation channels** (*zarcillos*) of a spin network.  
The framework includes **testable conjectures** on cosmic topology (e.g., the CMB Cold Spot) and clearly separates algebraic results, physical hypotheses, and speculative interpretations.

## 📜 Postulates

### I. Adimensional Ontology
Reality consists of adimensional points organised in a spin‑network graph \(\Gamma = (V,E)\) with finite‑dimensional local Hilbert spaces \(\mathcal{H}_n\). Spacetime is emergent.

### II. Modular Hamiltonian as Physical Hamiltonian
For each node \(n\) with faithful reduced state \(\rho_n\):
\[
H_n = k_B T_P \, K_{\rho_n}, \qquad K_{\rho_n} = -\ln \rho_n
\]
Physical time at node \(n\) is the local modular flow rescaled by \(t_P\).

### III. Informational Redistribution
Under unitary evolution of the global pure state \(|\Psi\rangle\), the **aggregate local modular energy** \(E_{\text{agg}} = \sum_n S(\rho_n)\) is an emergent functional that changes as entanglement is redistributed.  
Effective processes (diffusion, crystallization, BH‑WH cycles) approximately preserve \(E_{\text{agg}}\) over cosmological scales — a dynamical hypothesis, not a consequence of unitarity.

### IV. Dissipation Principle
The universe is the thermodynamic solution to dissipating maximal non‑crystallizable information without collapse, via:
- cosmic expansion,
- Higgs‑mediated materialization,
- fractal recursive black‑hole–white‑hole cycles.
